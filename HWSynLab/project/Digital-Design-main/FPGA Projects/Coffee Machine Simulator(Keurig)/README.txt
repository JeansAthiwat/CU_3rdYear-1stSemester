Coffee Machine Simulator aka Keurig machine
by David J. Marion

Inputs
btnL on BASYS 3 is to make coffee
btnR on BASYS 3 is to fill water
btnC on BASYS 3 is reset

Ouputs
16 LEDs on BASYS 3 to simulate cup filling with coffee
7 segment display:
	1. Number of cups remaining before needing to fill water
	2. When coffee is done
	3. When needing to fill water
